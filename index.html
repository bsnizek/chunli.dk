About Chunli

Chunli is a super cute Chinese researcher in the field of active travel. Born and raised in Inner Mongolia, China, she quickly moved to the Bicycle Kingdom of Denmark to pursue a career as a bicycle researcher. She is currently enrolled in the PhD programme of Copenhagen University.
